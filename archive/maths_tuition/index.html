Maths Tuition

We attract students from the South Gloucestershire education authority and surrounding areas. This includes both state and private school students.

We have over 10 years experience of identifying the specific needs of those who attend tuition and how to meet those needs.

We have worked with the syllabuses of all English and Welsh examination boards.

Primary

We begin with students as young as year 4.

They receive 1:1 teaching with a tutor who guides them through the understanding of the basic building blocks that make Maths work.

Coaching for Year 6 SATS is the high point of achievement – where appropriate for the level 6 test.

Post SATS we offer preparation for the move to Secondary school over the summer.

Secondary

From years 7 – 11 students can be accommodated in 1:1 tutoring or in small groups.

Intensive preparation for GCSE exams is a key element of our service.

We have undertaken tuition for all examination board syllabuses: AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC, iGCSE.

GCSE Re-take

Crash course available for November re-take exams to gain that elusive grade ‘C’.

Advanced Level

We have provided tuition across the examination board syllabuses: AQA, Edexcel, OCR, and the International Baccalaureate Diploma.

Tuition can be on a 1:1 or small group basis.

This helps students prepare for their entry to University or work placements.

And Beyond

We have also been providing tuition for post-18 education requirements.

For example:

Entry examinations for teacher training

Apprentice engineering courses

Nursing education requirements

On line aptitude tests for career development opportunities

And Finally

We offer the opportunity for parents to refresh their understanding on the basics of Mathematics. This means:

They are able to support their child at home

They understand how Maths has changed since “their days at school”

To retake GCSEs or even A levels
